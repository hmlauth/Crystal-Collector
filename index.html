Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

Using the four crystals below, each with a hidden value, click on any one to discover it's value;each click will add to your current total score.

Win by matching your total score to the random number. Lose by exceeding the random number.

Remember... Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal change too!
